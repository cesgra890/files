The pack of two Edge Animate projects bundles:

  1. Spinning SVG Earth Globe Flat (33 presets)
  2. Spinning SVG Earth Globe 3D (11 presets)

Features:

  • Edge Animate projects
  • Resolution independent scalable vector graphics (all graphics are SVG files)
  • Easy to customize colors, opacity of elements (with or without Edge Animate software), or just remove them
  • Works on desktop and mobile devices
  • Included:
    • Edge Animate files (.an, JS, HTML, SVG)
    • Edge Animate templates (.antmpl)
    • Documentations
    • PSD helpers

1. Spinning SVG Earth Globe Flat (33 presets)

  • Small size of vector graphics (on average, 26KB size for uncompressed SVG and 8KB – for compressed)
  • In addition each of the preset has two versions:
    • globe with five continents (without Antarctica)
    • globe with six continents
  • Speed: easy to change speed of animation
  • 33 presets of the globe


2. Spinning SVG Earth Globe 3D (11 presets)

  • Loop of smooth animation based on 90, 180 or 360 frames spritesheet
  • 3D globe simulation
  • Globe with six continents (with Antarctica)
  • Speed: fixed, 30 frames per second animation
  • 11 presets of the globe
